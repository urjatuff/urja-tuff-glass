What we manufacture

Two products, built to spec.

Every order is cut, processed and tested to the dimensions you need — no minimum size, no maximum project.

Toughened Glass

Tempered safety glass

Heat-treated to be several times stronger than standard annealed glass. Breaks into small, blunt granules instead of sharp shards — built for doors, railings, partitions, and façades.

  • Thickness5mm – 19mm
  • Max sizeCustom on request
  • Use casesDoors, windows, railings
DGU Glass

Double glazed units

Two panes of glass separated by a sealed air or gas gap, cutting heat transfer and outside noise. Assembled with a desiccant-filled spacer for a fog-free, insulated unit.

  • ConfigurationPane + gap + pane
  • BenefitThermal & acoustic insulation
  • Use casesFaçades, cold zones, offices

How it's made

From raw sheet to finished pane.

Two distinct lines run through our factory in Bhagwanpur — one for tempering, one for insulated assembly.

01

Cut & edge

Raw glass is cut to size and edges ground smooth to prevent stress points.

02

Heat treat

Sheets pass through a furnace and are heated close to their softening point.

03

Rapid quench

High-pressure air cools the surface fast, locking in compressive strength.

04

Quality test

Each batch is checked for stress distribution, flatness and clarity.

01

Pane selection

Two panes are matched for thickness and type based on the spec.

02

Spacer & desiccant

An aluminium spacer filled with desiccant is set between the panes.

03

Seal

The unit is sealed air-tight with primary and secondary sealants.

04

Inspect

Units are checked for seal integrity and fog-free clarity.
